Support:
  * Chrome, FF, Safari 5.1+
  * iOS 4.3+, Chrome for Android

Technology used:
  * Compass/ SaSS
  * flexbox (slide layouts)
  * transitions/transforms/animations
  * RequireJS
  * window.postMessage() for speaker mode
